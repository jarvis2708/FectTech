Keyword Research Tools

Finding the right keywords is the foundation of SEO. Here are the best tools:

Ahrefs

Premium

One of the most comprehensive keyword research tools with massive backlink data.

Key Features:

  • Keyword Explorer with 10B+ keywords
  • Site Explorer for competitor analysis
  • Content Explorer for content ideas
  • Rank tracking
From $99/month

SEMrush

Premium

All-in-one marketing toolkit with excellent keyword and competitor analysis.

Key Features:

  • Keyword Magic Tool
  • Position Tracking
  • Site Audit
  • Social Media Toolkit
From $120/month

Google Keyword Planner

Free

Free tool from Google with accurate search volume data.

Key Features:

  • Accurate search volumes
  • Keyword ideas
  • CPC data for ads
  • Integrated with Google Ads
Free
